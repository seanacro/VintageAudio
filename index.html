Experienced Vintage Audio Repair

We can service many of the top vintage audio systems, such as Marantz, McIntosh, and others

Who are we?

Since 1980, we have been servicing top home audio brands both in and out of warranty. If you have a vintage piece of gear from the 70s or 80s, chances are we have been working on it and other pieces like it since it was new!

Receivers, Turntables, Reel to Reel players, and more can be given new life for many more years of enjoyment.

Since we have been the regional, factory authorized service center for many years, we have access to parts, service manuals, and training like no other. Our skill and experience means we diagnose and repair equipment down to the component level.
